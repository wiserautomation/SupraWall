## 💡 TIPS FOR FASTER PROCESSING

1. **Work in batches:** Process 4-5 URLs at a time, then check back 5 minutes later
2. **Parallel windows:** Open GSC in one window, checklist in another
3. **Copy/paste:** Pre-copy URLs to clipboard for faster input
4. **Don't wait for 100%:** You don't need to wait for each test to complete before starting the next URL
5. **Track time:** Note the time for each URL to estimate total batch time
