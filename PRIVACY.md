# SupraWall Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 2026**

SupraWall ("we", "our", or "us") provides a deterministic security layer for AI agents. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our website, tools, or integrations.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** Email address, name, and billing information when you register for an account.
- **Usage Data:** Telemetry from our SDKs strictly related to policy evaluation (e.g., tool names, policy outcomes, timestamps). By default, we **do not** log raw argument payloads unless explicitly configured to do so for compliance auditing.
- **Log Data:** IP addresses, browser type, and interaction metrics on our dashboard.

## 2. How We Use the Information
- To provide and maintain the SupraWall service.
- To enforce AI agent policies deterministically (ALLOW, DENY, REQUIRE_APPROVAL).
- To generate compliance logs and audit trails as requested by the user.
- To notify you about changes to our service or security updates.

## 3. Data Processing and AI Frameworks
SupraWall acts as a middleware processor. We integrate with frameworks like LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen, and Vercel AI SDK. We **do not** use your data or your agents' prompt/response payloads to train our models. All data processed through SupraWall remains your property. 

## 4. EU AI Act Compliance & GDPR
We are committed to helping our customers achieve compliance with the EU AI Act and GDPR. SupraWall provides features like PII Shielding to prevent sensitive data from leaving your environment. If you require a Data Processing Agreement (DPA), please contact our privacy team.

## 5. Third-Party Access
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners.
